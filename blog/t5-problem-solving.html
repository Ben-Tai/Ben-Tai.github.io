Tell your non-tech friend a story about a time you:

1. Got blocked on a simple problem

What was the problem? I got stuck on trying to a the list to line up underneath another list.

What problem solving techniques did you use? Tried to figure it out myself, than I looked up a youtube video

How did you feel throughout the process? Frustrated and confused

What did you learn? That you have to be sure that you have the correct youtube video, and even then you dont have the options for troubleshooting if it doesnt like work like it did in the video

2. Solved a problem in an elegant way?

What was the problem? I got stuck on the chessboard and getting it to create a space on the 2nd line.

What problem solving techniques did you use? I read through the hints and then googled the challenge.

How did you feel throughout the process? Confused at first but relieved to understand where I was going wrong and how the solution worked.

What did you learn? That alot of the answers to different coding challenges can be found online.
