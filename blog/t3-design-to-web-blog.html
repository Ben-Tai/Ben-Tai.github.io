What is responsive site, and why is responsiveness important?

A responisve site will move things on the website that your visiting so that it looks good no matter what size screen your using like a big screen on a computer or a small screen on your phone.

What is mobile first design, and why it's important?

Mobile first design, means to design your webiste for mobile first and then build your styling to change as the screen gets bigger.
This is important you need to make sure that your crucial content will fit on a small screen, and its then easier to add more as the screen gets bigger giving you more room to work with.

What are frameworks, what are the pros and cons when using them?

A framework is a pre-designed structure to build your website upon.
The pros are that it makes it alot easier and quicker to build something that will look great.
The cons are that it can be difficult sometimes to add other elements to the code as you then have to alter the structure.

What is a wireframe and why do we use it?

A wireframe is a mock-up of your page layout and design
We use them to give you an clear direction of how to build your webiste and were tp place your content.
