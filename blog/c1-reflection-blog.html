Sprint 1

Reflections after watching Shareef's fireside chat

9th December 2017

What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience?

Im impressed and alittle surprised at the rate in which DBC grew in its first 2years and too now be in NZ shows that there is quite a high demand for this style of education. But i think that the programme is aptly named a 'bootcamp' for a reason, as it is meant to be a 'transformation' almost, involving alot of hard work and sacrifice.

What are your impressions?

I'm encouraged that I will be travelling down this journey with similar mined and positioned peers and supportive teachers, and am aware that it will involve alot of hardwork.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I'm a pretty social person and a pretty hardworker so I hope to make lot's of friends and can see myself being kicked out when setting the alarms.

Have your expectations for EDA changed?

Not really.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning enviroment?

Yes I am excited, and yes I am a little nervous. Deffinitely sharing one of those common fears of DBC beginners.
